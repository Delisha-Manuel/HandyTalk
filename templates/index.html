HandyTalk

A real-time American Sign Language translator using computer vision and machine learning.

The Problem:

Did you know ASL is the third most common language in the world, after English and Spanish?

Globally, over 1.5 billion people have some form hearing loss, with 430 million having disabling hearing loss. The Hearing Loss Association of America estimates that around 48 million Americans have some hearing loss, with numbers increasing with age, especially for those over 65. This means daily activities like conversations with friends are inaccessible millions of people!

The Solution:

Because of this, HandyTalk allows people to spell out words to each other in sign language, verifying each one. Just hold up your letter, and the MediaPipe neural network will work predict what you are saying!
